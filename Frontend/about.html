About us

The Sketch2UML approach

Sketch2UML is an end-to-end sketch recognition approach for UML Diagrams that is currently under submission. Sketch2UML uses a neural network-based architecture to recognize the shapes, edges, and labels of hand-drawn UML Diagrams. Converting these hand-drawn sketches, particularly UML (Unified Modeling Language) diagrams, into digital formats poses a significant challenge. This manual conversion process is time-consuming and labor-intensive, hindering efficiency and collaboration in fast-paced work environments.
